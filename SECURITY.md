# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities privately by email to
jperlow@gmail.com. Do not open a public issue for security-sensitive reports.

Include the affected version, a concise description, reproduction steps or proof
of concept where possible, and any logs or configuration details needed to
validate the issue.

We will acknowledge reports as quickly as practical, investigate in good faith,
and coordinate disclosure timing with the reporter when a fix or mitigation is
available.
